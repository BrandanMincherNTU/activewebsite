About Us

Welcome to the Active Events website. Active Events will serve you as a hub for many different types of outdoors events in the United Kingdom. At Active Events we strive publisise and advertise outdoors running events that meet the desires of our users, whether you are just beginning your fitness journey and are a complete beginner to verteran runners already participating in such events across the United Kingdom we aim to provide you with an irreplacable service. Our Mission at Active Events is to inspire people to get out there in their local communties and meet others with similar interests and hobbies through our events. Active Events will be with you throughout the entire way offering our services on the website, where you can also contact us directly with any questions you may have, we aim to make your steps to becoming a more active individuals or keeping up with your activities as seemless as possible. Now you know what we are all about at Active Events feel free to further browse the website to see if any of our events peek your interests.

How To Use The Website

The Active Events Website is designed specifically to make it easily understood and simple so that anyone can navigate and find exactly what they are looking for, if your would like to know more about how to use the website and learn each part of the website this section is for you! You can navigate around the website using the Navigation Bar at the top of the website which has multiple buttons for sending you to different parts of the website and user controls such as darkmode. Firstly we have the home page (you are currently on) this is our websites landing page which contains information about us at Active Events, Website Information and a highlighted event so users can have an example of what type of events we promote on the website. Next is the events page which will list many of the events being held in the coming future. Finally is the contacts page where you can fill out a form to contact us the website owners. We hope you have a good time on our website and find everything to your liking, if you have any concerns or suggested changes or features you would like on our website please contact us.
