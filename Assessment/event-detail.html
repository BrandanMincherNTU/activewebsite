Description:

A Half Marathon that starts in Sheffield city centre and takes a route through the peak district and finishes back at Sheffield city centre. Throughout the race there will be facilities to aid you in your run. Entrants must be 17 years or older. Thousands of runners will be taking part with a wide variety of skill and experience and the staff and participants are welcoming for all those willing to participate. The event fundraiser for many different local causes such as Sheffield Hospitals Chairty, Macmillan cancer support and St Lukes Sheffield Hospice. The Sheffield Half Marathon has amassed large crowds for cheering on the runners so you won't be lacking in support throughout your race, so what are you waiting for!
